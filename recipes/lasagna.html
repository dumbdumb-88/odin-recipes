Lasagna Recipe

Description

This delicious dish is a staple of any italian american household. Brought over the sea in 1865 by Louis Versaccia, it quickly took over the culinary scene.

In those days, times were tough for the average Italian immigrant. Rejected by the general populace, italian food was not the staple it is today. Nowadays you can't go anywhere without finding an Olive Garden or a local Pizzeria!

Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagna Noodles uncooked
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese shredded and divided
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese shredded

Tomato Sauce

  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 36 ounces pasta sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
