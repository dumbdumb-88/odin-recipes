Rice Cakes

Description

Rice cakes are a delectable treat for anyone with an asian background. Many Asian-Americans love the delightful blandness and adaptability of the rice cake.

While a staple for Asian-Americans, many non immigrants cannot cook these tasty treats. Today we will be breaking down this dish, in all of its simple yet wonderful detail!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1 tbsp warm water
  • 1/8 tsp oil
  • oil to grease pan
  • water for steamer

Steps

  1. Dissolve the sugar in the water by heating it in a small pot. Set aside to cool down to room temperature
  2. Mix the yeast with warm water. Set it aside to bloom(usually a few minutes).
  3. Add the rice,flour,and sugar water to a large bowl. Mix until well combined. Add in the yeast mixture and lightly stir to combine. Cover adn let it proof for 2-3 hours in a warm enviroment. Add a few drops of oil 30 minutes into the proofing step
  4. Once a thick layer of small bubbles are on the surface of the rice mixture,lightly grease a 9-inch pie pan.Then add the rice mixture to the pan.
  5. Boil the water for the steamer first. Add the pan to the steamer. Cover with the lid and let it steam for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Remove the pan from the steamers and let it completely cool down. Remove the cake from the pan and slice into cubes. Serve and enjoy!
